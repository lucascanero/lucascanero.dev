~/about

About

I started on the creative side of technology — motion design, 3D and sound — studying Audiovisual & Multimedia at the Polytechnic Institute of Beja. Somewhere between rendering frames and mixing audio, I got more interested in the systems underneath: the pipelines, the servers, the automation that makes digital work possible.

Since 2022 I've been a DevOps engineer at Montblu, a consultancy that runs infrastructure for other companies. My main client today is FilingRamp, where I design and operate AWS infrastructure end to end — Terraform, Kubernetes (EKS), CI/CD and observability — while also working across Azure and Google Cloud on other projects.

The two backgrounds aren't separate. Design taught me that the best systems are the ones you don't notice — and I bring that eye to everything from dashboards to internal tooling. Long term, I want to build scalable, reliable cloud infrastructure that enables teams to move faster..
